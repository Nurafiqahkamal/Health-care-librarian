Librarian Policy

As the Health Librarian at the Smart HealthCare Information Hub, I am committed to upholding professional values, ethical standards, and responsible information management practices. The following policy outlines my roles and responsibilities in delivering high-quality, trustworthy, and inclusive library services.

1. Professional Ethics

  • Ensure accurate and up-to-date health information is accessible to all users.
  • Respect user confidentiality, intellectual property, and copyright laws.
  • Promote equity, inclusion, and respect in information delivery.

2. Information Management

  • Curate reliable health resources from credible sources such as WHO, government health portals, and peer-reviewed journals.
  • Ensure materials are reviewed and updated regularly to maintain relevancy and accuracy.
  • Organize digital collections using metadata standards to support easy discovery.

3. User Support & Communication

  • Provide assistance through chat, email, and consultation to users seeking medical information.
  • Educate users on how to search, evaluate, and use health resources responsibly.
  • Respond promptly to inquiries while maintaining a respectful and professional tone.

4. Technology Integration

  • Use tools such as AI Chatbot, ebooks, appointment systems, and video libraries to enhance user engagement.
  • Ensure all digital platforms are accessible, mobile-friendly, and user-centered.
  • Maintain and improve website content and features based on user feedback.

5. Continuous Learning & Development

  • Stay updated with the latest trends in digital librarianship, health informatics, and ICT in libraries.
  • Participate in professional development, webinars, and health-related conferences.
  • Encourage collaboration with other librarians, healthcare professionals, and educators.

6. Feedback and Improvement

  • Users are encouraged to provide suggestions to enhance library services.
  • All feedback will be used constructively to improve digital resources and user experience.
